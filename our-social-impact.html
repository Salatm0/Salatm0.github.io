Our Mission

Our mission is to make music accessible to all, empowering communities through its transformative power.

Vision

We envision a world where everyone has equal access to music education and opportunities, fostering stronger, more connected communities.

Why We Matter

Washington State's music and entertainment ecosystem directly employs roughly 49,200 people, supporting 74,700 jobs and generating about $6.3 billion in GDP. Despite this, 64% of independent musicians cite financial constraints as their top challenge, and 78% earned less than $15,000 from music in the past year. Above The Record is building the infrastructure to change that.

About Us

Above The Record (ATR) is a Black-owned, woman-owned music and entertainment company founded by Teryn Jaziré Reché, based in Tacoma, WA. ATR emerged from the grassroots and independent music community, where artists historically lacked access to performance opportunities, financial support, and industry infrastructure. Our foundation is officially set up under Shunpike. We develop and book artists, produce dynamic live events, and deliver cutting-edge music business education — connecting talent, partners, and communities across the Pacific Northwest and beyond.

Community Programs

Above The Record hosts and produces live music events, showcases, and community experiences across the Pacific Northwest — creating space for artists and audiences to connect.
