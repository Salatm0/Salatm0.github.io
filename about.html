About Above The Record

Above the Record (ATR) is a Black-owned, woman-owned music and entertainment company founded by Teryn Jaziré Reché, based in Tacoma, WA. ATR emerged from the grassroots and independent music community, where artists historically lacked access to performance opportunities, financial support, and industry infrastructure.

ATR functions as more than a traditional label. It develops and books artists, produces dynamic live events, and delivers cutting-edge music business education. By 2026, the goal is to evolve into a full-service label — providing the infrastructure, resources, and partnerships artists need to thrive in today's global music industry.

Washington State's music and entertainment ecosystem directly employs roughly 49,200 people, supporting 74,700 jobs, generating about $6.3 billion in GDP and nearly $10 billion in total economic impact. Despite this, 64% of independent musicians cite financial constraints as their top challenge, and 78% earned less than $15,000 from music in the past year.

ATR is building the infrastructure to change that — connecting talent, partners, and communities through scalable programs, strategic partnerships, and impactful live entertainment across the Pacific Northwest and beyond.

Our Mission

Above The Record is a fast-growing music and entertainment company building artists, experiences, and industry pathways that create jobs, expand opportunity, and drive sustainable growth across the music economy.

Our Vision

To grow Above The Record into a globally recognized music and entertainment enterprise — standing alongside industry leaders such as Sony Music and Warner Music Group — while building inclusive, future-forward infrastructure that shapes the next generation of artists, executives, and creative businesses.
