Our Mission

Our mission is to make music accessible to all, empowering communities through its transformative power.

Vision

We envision a world where everyone has equal access to music education and opportunities, fostering stronger, more connected communities.

Why We Matter

In the U.S., 4.5 million young people aspire to engage with music but face obstacles. Music 4 Everybody is dedicated to breaking down these barriers so that music can reach and benefit all.

About Us

Founded in 2022 by the Marshall Law Band, we are a non-profit focused on removing barriers to music in underserved communities. We offer free events, career development opportunities for artists, and partner with major companies to support education and overcome challenges. Based in King County, we're expanding our impact across the Pacific Northwest.
